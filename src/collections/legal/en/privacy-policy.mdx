# Privacy Policy

## Data Controller

- Identity: Inmoedit
- Contact Email: info@inmoedit.com
- Data Protection Office Contact: You can contact the Data Protection Office via email at info@inmoedit.com.

## Purpose of Personal Data Processing

Your personal data will be processed for the following purposes:

- Managing the registration process on our website.
- Managing content distribution lists.
- Responding to your contact or information request.
- Sending you information about our products and services, including by electronic means.
- Analyzing website traffic.

We will not use your personal information for any other purpose than those mentioned above, which you expressly accept.

## Category of Data Processed

The personal data we use is obtained from the information you provide when filling out online forms. Specifically, we will use the following data if you complete the available form:

- Identification Data: name, surname(s), company, and email address.

## Data Retention Period

Your personal data will be retained for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. We understand that, after this period, your data may become outdated and you may no longer be interested in working with Inmoedit, so we will proceed to delete all your information.

## Legal Basis for Data Processing

We only process users' data if we have the users' express and informed consent to do so, and always in accordance with the purposes described in the purpose specification section.

## Communication of Personal Data

The personal data you provide to us may be communicated to entities within the Inmoedit group. We guarantee that, if your data leaves the European Economic Area, it will be treated with the same level of protection applicable in Spain. For this, we have the necessary licenses and contracts to ensure the security of your data according to the highest standards.

## User Rights Over Their Personal Data

You have the following rights over your personal data:

- Right to request access to personal data.
- Right to request rectification or deletion.
- Right to object.
- Right to request the limitation of processing.
- Right to data portability.

To exercise your rights, you must send us a written communication via email to info@inmoedit.com, attaching a copy of your ID and details of the right you wish to exercise. We also inform you that you can request the protection of your rights before the Spanish Data Protection Agency at Calle de Jorge Juan, 6, 28001 Madrid.

## Modifications to the Privacy Policy

Inmoedit reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be duly published on our website.
This Privacy Policy is updated as of July 31, 2024.
